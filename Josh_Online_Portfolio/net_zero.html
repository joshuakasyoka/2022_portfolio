We were tasked with helping define next steps for the product ‘Moata Net Zero’ - a carbon reporting tool which would bring together the pockets of excellence being demonstrated across Moata and the wider industry into a common offering

We wanted to build on the team’s existing market investigations, validate the understanding that had been synthesised and provide further stimulus material for MNZ to provoke deeper investigation and help identify the opportunity areas and product-market fit.
